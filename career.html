Business Continuity Analyst


Business Continuity Analysts are the disaster relief experts in the IT industry. Business continuity analysts ensure that companies recover data and resume normal operations following blackouts or disasters. Business continuity analysts research and prepare recovery strategies based upon possible risks and potential losses. Business continuity analysts are leaders with an extensive knowledge of information security and risk management who also train employees within businesses to execute recovery plans in the event of a disaster.
